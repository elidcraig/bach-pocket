CLEF NOTES

A BRIEF INTRODUCTION TO A FEW FAVORITE MUSICAL INSTRUMENTS

Piano

A large keyboard musical instrument with a wooden case enclosing a soundboard and metal strings, which are struck by hammers when the keys are depressed. The strings' vibration is stopped by dampers when the keys are released and can be regulated for length and volume by two or three pedals.

Guitar

A stringed musical instrument, with a fretted fingerboard, typically incurved sides, and six or twelve strings, played by plucking or strumming with the fingers or a pick.

Cello

A bass instrument of the violin family, held upright on the floor between the legs of the seated player.

Timpani

A large drum shaped like a bowl, with a membrane adjustable for tension (and so pitch) stretched across.

Trumpet

A brass musical instrument with a flared bell and a bright, penetrating tone. The modern instrument has the tubing looped to form a straight-sided coil, with three valves.
